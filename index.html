Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Naive Bayes Classifier work?
Our diagnostic checker uses a local Naive Bayes probabilistic model. It computes the posterior probability of each condition based on your selected symptoms and the pre-defined clinical dataset. By using Bayes' theorem, it calculates how likely a disease is given the symptoms, displaying a step-by-step mathematical breakdown for transparency.
Is my medical data stored securely?
Yes, completely. This application runs entirely within your browser client. No symptoms, reports, or login details are uploaded to any external server. All diagnostic history and custom condition configurations are stored in your browser's local storage (`localStorage`).
What do the Clinical Triage Urgency levels mean?
Each diagnostic result is assigned an urgency level:
  • Emergency Care (Red): High-risk symptoms detected. Immediate emergency medical assistance is recommended.
  • Urgent Care (Orange): Moderate to high severity symptoms. Visit a clinic or consult a physician promptly.
  • Clinic Care (Yellow): Standard symptoms. Non-urgent clinical consultation or primary physician visit.
  • Self-Care (Green): Low severity symptoms. Monitor status and practice standard home care.
How do I download my diagnostic PDF report?
After completing a symptom check, go to your Personal Dashboard. Under the "Saved Diagnostic Reports" list, click the PDF icon next to any record to download a professionally formatted clinical report featuring a custom triage colored banner.
